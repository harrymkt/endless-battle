About The Game

Endless Battle (short for EB) is an offline 3d FPS shooting game for the people with visual impairment, nothing but using a screen reader.

The game is plaied in a 3d environment world, where your task is to kill the enemies that spawned at every few seconds. As you kill the enemies, you gain coins. These coins can be used to buy ammos and health related items in the store.

Features

  • Screen reader support.
  • Audio, no visuals.
  • Providing endless hours of game play until you stop, as the name suggests.
  • Innovative quality head related transform function (IQHRTF) is supported for the accurate 3d sounds experience.
  • Supported Windows, Linux.
  • Automatically gain new weapons as you increase your resurrection.
  • Provided more than 4 different weapons, each being with different strength and quality.
  • Provided more than 2 enemy types to fight, each with its own set of strength, damage, and quality.
  • Never lose your game progress as it automatically saves the data.
